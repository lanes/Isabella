The Product

We got a recent shipment of Kaffe Fassett‘s Shot Cotton, and the deep hues made me think autumn. Shot cotton is special because the warp and weft yarns are 2 different colors woven together to create a special depth, a ‘shot‘ of color. When the edge of this fabric ravels, you can see a bright contrast to the body of the fabric.
